- Giving too much room to stock thinking i am taking positional trade, Don't do this it's not working out for you it's giving high drawdown when market is weak, best solution is to just stick to swing trading don't try two separate system. example:
- Selling too early,wait for stock to do some violation before selling, if you feel that it's extended then again wait for next day morning to sell and trail with 5 min chart
