## :fontawesome-solid-book: Books

### General Books
- How I Made $2,000,000 in the Stock Market Book by Nicolas Darvas
    - Good book for getting started
    - To figure out what's core requirement for trading
- Zurich Axioms by Max Gunther
    - this book will teach you importance of taking risk
    - why and how to take calculated risks
- How Legendary traders made million by john boik
    - this book will explain entire history of us stock market
    - why market fall and rise the way it did during each decade

### Trading Books
- Trade like a Stock Market Wizard by Mark Minervini
    - This book is core of whatever i am doing for my momentum trading
    - I am using this same setups and psycology and risk management
- Think and Trade like a champion by Mark minervini
- How to Make money in stock by william O Neil
    - this is first orginal book written for momentum traders
    - most of things written is this book is still affective in current market
- The Perfect Speculator by Brad Koteshwar
    - this book mainly explain how to speculate and figure out big trends
    - this book really resonate with whatever i am trying to do
- The Perfect stock by Brad Koteshwar
    - similar to perfect speculator with bit more examples
- How to Trade in Stocks by Jesse livermore
    - really old book but very aggresive one for momentum trading
