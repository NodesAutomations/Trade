# Free Float

## Overview
- Free float is the number of shares of a stock that are available for trading in the market
- It is calculated by subtracting the number of shares held by promoters, insiders, and other restricted shareholders from the total number of shares outstanding
- Free float value is a measure of the liquidity and market capitalization of a stock. 
- A higher free float value indicates that there are more shares available for trading, which can lead to better price discovery and less volatility.
