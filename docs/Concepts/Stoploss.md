# Stoploss

## Initial Stoploss
- Use Previous Swing Low or day's low depending on setup and market
- Move to trailing stoploss when 10/21 Day moving average is above your stoploss
> When you take entry in morning make sure that you have 2-3% of profit cushion by end of day. If you don't have profit cushion by end of day or stock have weak closing then it's better to sell full/half with small loss.

## Trailing Stoploss
- When Market is strong
    - Use 10/21 Day MA as stoploss but give 3-4% extra space below MA to deal with shakeouts
    - Some stocks follow 10D MA and some follow 21D MA, so use your personal instinct for this
- When Market is weak and you're looking to sell
    - Use previous day low as trailing stoploss

## Stoploss based on Market Conditions
- this is like sub category of stoploss
- Current market aware ness is also taken into consideration for this
- when market is overbought and you're expecting pull back swich to sell stoploss
- when market is oversold but there's still lot of volatility then stick with hard stoploss even when you're in good profit
- Hard Stoploss
    - When i don't want to chock my trade but want to protect my capital from worst case scenarios
    - Hard stoploss should not trigger for daily price volatility
    - normally taken considering price action of day chart
- Sell Stoploss
    - when i've already decided to take exit due to negative price action or want to protect major chuch of my profit
    - soft stoploss will trigger when there's sudden fall or high volatility 
    - normally taken on smaller time frame 5/15 min or on previous day's low
