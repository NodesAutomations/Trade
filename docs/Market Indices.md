## Broad Market Indices

- Refer : [Broad based Indices (niftyindices.com)](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/broad-based-indices)
- Nifty 50
    - The Nifty 50 is a well diversified 50 stock index and it represent important sectors of the economy.
- Nifty Next 50
    - The Nifty Next 50 Index represents 50 companies from Nifty 100 after excluding the Nifty 50 companies.

## Sectorial Index

- [**Nifty Auto**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-auto)
    - Represent Automobile Sectors
- [**Nifty Bank**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-bank)
    - Represent Banking Sector
- [**Nifty Financial Services**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-financial-services)
    - reflect the behaviour and performance of the Indian financial market which includes banks, financial institutions, housing finance, insurance companies and other financial services companies.
- [**Nifty FMCG**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-fmcg)
    - incudes companies that deals with those goods and products, which are non-durable, mass consumption products and available off the shelf.
- [**Nifty IT**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-it)
    - Represent all it companies
- [**Nifty Media**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-media)
    - Media & Entertainment sector including printing and publishing in India.
- [**Nifty Metal**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-metal)
    - reflect the behavior and performance of the metals sector including mining in India.
- [**Nifty Pharma**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-pharma)
    - reflect the behavior and performance of the pharmaceutical sector in India.
- [**Nifty Private Bank**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-private-bank)
    - Represent all private banks
- [**Nifty PSU Bank**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-psu-bank)
    - Represent all public banks
- [**Nifty Realty**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-realty)
    - real estate companies that are primarily engaged into construction of residential and commercial properties.
- [**Nifty Consumer Durables**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-consumer-durables-index)
- [**Nifty Oil and Gas**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/sectoral-indices/nifty-oil-and-gas-index)
    - reflect the performance of the stocks belonging to Oil, Gas and Petroleum industry.
- [**Nifty Infrastructure**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/thematic-indices/nifty-infrastructure)
    - representing the infrastructure sector which includes companies belonging to Telecom, Power, Port, Air, Roads, Railways, shipping and other Utility Services providers.
- [**Nifty Energy**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/thematic-indices/nifty-energy)
    - Petroleum, Gas and Power
- [**Nifty IPO**](https://www.niftyindices.com/indices/equity/thematic-indices/nifty-ipo)
  - represent preformance of Last 100 IPOs listed on NSE.
