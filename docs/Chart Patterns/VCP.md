### Overview
- Volatility contraction pattern was developed by Mark Minervini
- It is an extended cup with handle
- Volatility should contract from left to right
- Ideally each subsequent wave of price correction should be approximately half of the previous correction
- VCP can be horizonal or decline or incline

### When to Enter
- On breakout of trend from VCP trendline
