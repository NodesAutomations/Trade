### Stage 1 Vs Stage 3

- stage 1 happen after bear market
- stage 3 happen after bull market
- in stage 3 we see pattern like  h&s or double or triple top
- In stage 1, we might see pattern like inverted h & s or double or tiple bottom
- the 200 day ema would be above the price in stage 1 and below the price in stage 3
