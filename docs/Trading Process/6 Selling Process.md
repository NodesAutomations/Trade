# How to Sell a Stock

## Overview
- Always use limit order for selling, don't ever use market order, except for emergency
- Use GTT SL for everything, never trade without Stoploss
- Don't wait till EOD to sell, Sell as soon as stock hit your stoploss, If stock recover well with good volume you can buy it back but if stock didn't recover then you will lost another 2-5% beyound your stoploss which will affect your stats

## Selling Process depending on different scenarios

### When you buy stock breaking out of base
- VCP, CWH are base breakout patterns
- Use Swing low as intial SL
- Trail your stoploss, don't choke your trade

### When you buy Uptrending stock
- bull flag, bull pennant, high tight flag, flat base are continuation pattern
- For this type of brakout don't wait for stock to break down, Just sell into strength when stock looks extended, or use tight stoploss to lock in profit

### When Stock is extended
- Move your SL to extended day low or 10Day whichever is higher
- Don't chock your trade, stock can go extended for multiple days
- you can also book 50% if you're holding very large position to reduce risk. 
- For regular position don't sell half, because it will increase number of stocks in your portfolio

### When Earning Results is due
- Never hold a stock through earnings if you don't have enough cushion to protect against potential downside.
- When Market is strong
    - if you have 10%+ profit cushion and related industry group performance is also strong then and then only hold through earning
    - If stocks within the same industry group have generally reacted well to earnings, it may potentially be an indicator that it is safer to hold through earnings.
- When Market is Weak
    - Always sell 1 day before earning
    - Buy back if results are good and Market response is positive

!!! Warning
    *"earnings events are binary events and there are no guarantees. You must be willing to give up potential gains or face losses."*

### When Market is weak
- When index is in short downtrend
- When market breath is looking very weak
- then it's good idea to tighen your stoploss and lock in some profit

### When you buy stock in morning and have weak closing
- first check underlying index for that stock, if it's looking good no need to do anything
- if not you need to limit your losses by either reducing position size or keep tight stoploss for next day. you can set fix 1-2% stoploss or set stoploss at previous 5 min swing low

### When Stock squat after big gain
- Sell half if stock loose most of it's gain, sell full if it goes below day's low
