### What it is
- Pivot point buy means when stock breakout of specific trend
- this can be range breakout after tight consolidation 
- this can also be breakout from normal trendline after tight consolidation and multiple resistance
- Make sure that stock is above 10/21 day moving averages so General buy rules still applies here
- One more thing to keep in mind that only focus on low risk setup in this category, if you missed your entry just ignore it

### Why you should try it instead of regular breakouts
- Low risk entry so better Risk reward
- Less volatility because not many eyes are on this spot
- Pyramiding  positions becomes much easier
