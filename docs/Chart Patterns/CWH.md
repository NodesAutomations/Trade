### Overview
- Cup with handle pattern was developed by William O Neil
- One of the best chart patterns, Yet one of most  misinterpreted
- It's supposed to form during uptrend, it's basically 2 wave VCP
- The Handle represent a shakeout where weak holders sell
- It is preferable if the base is round and not in a V shape
- A lot of cup with handle suggest beginning of bull market
